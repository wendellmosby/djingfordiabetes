Why We Exist

Every movement begins with one story.

When our son Myles was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes, our family entered a world we never expected.

We quickly learned that managing diabetes requires courage every single day. But we also discovered something equally important: joy belongs in the treatment plan.

DJing for Diabetes was born from one simple belief: every child deserves moments of celebration — not just on birthdays, but throughout their diabetes journey.

“Diabetes changed our lives. Joy changed our perspective.”

How We Create Joy

Music. Milestones. Memories.

🎧 Celebrate

We transform diagnosis anniversaries into joyful milestones of courage, strength, and community.

🎁 Encourage

We create celebration kits that remind children and families they are seen, supported, and never alone.

🌍 Connect

We bring together families, DJs, healthcare professionals, sponsors, and communities around the world.

Meet the Inspiration

“Diabetes became part of our story — but it never became the whole story.”

Myles’ journey inspired a foundation built on celebration, courage, and the belief that children living with diabetes deserve more than management — they deserve moments of joy.
