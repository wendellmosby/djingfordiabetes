Why We Exist

It started with one little boy.

When our son Myles was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes, our family entered a new world of insulin, alarms, blood sugar checks, uncertainty, and courage.

But we made a decision: diabetes would be part of his story, not the whole story.

DJing for Diabetes was created to celebrate resilience, create unforgettable memories, and remind every child and family that joy belongs in the treatment plan.

“Diabetes changed our lives. Joy changed our perspective.”
