Our Mission

DJing for Diabetes Foundation empowers and uplifts individuals and families affected by Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes through the universal language of music.

What We’re Building

Diaversary Celebrations

Helping families celebrate diabetes milestones with music, joy, and encouragement.

Diaversary Kits

Creating celebration kits that help children feel seen, supported, and celebrated.

The Global Joy Tour

Expanding D4D’s mission internationally through partnerships, storytelling, and community connection.

Our Big Goal

We are on a mission to celebrate 100,000 children living with diabetes through joy, music, and community by 2035.
